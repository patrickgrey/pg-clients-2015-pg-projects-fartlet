Fartlets!

This app allows you to listen to and watch a Fartlek countdown to time your runs. You can adjust the time for each fartlek and the number of sets.

Drag the buttons at the edge of the circles to change settings. Press the big button to start running.

Developer note: I call Fartleks Fartlets because I'm incurably immature.
